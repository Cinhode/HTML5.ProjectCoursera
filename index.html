Some Anime Waifu

About

A waifu is a fictional character a fan considers their ideal life partner. The term itself is a Japanization of the English word "wife," and was first used in an anime. "Waifu" is typically used to refer to anime characters, but can be used for other fictional characters as well, such as those in movies, TV shows, or books.
